Chicken Salad recipe

A simple balanced meal you can throw together in 10 minutes. Perfect for busy days.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Black beans
  • Cucumbers
  • Lettuce
  • Corn
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Avocado
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Slice the chicken breast into strips, cook on medium to high heat for about 5 minutes on each side.
  2. Dice the avocado, cucumber and tomato.
  3. Slice the onion and lettuce.
  4. Drain the corn and beans and wash them unless you aren't using canned ones.
  5. Assemble the salad; add all ingredients to a bowl.
  6. Squeeze lime on top according to your taste preferences.
